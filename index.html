Rules

Basic Rules of Blackjack:
  • Player and Dealer are dealt two cards. Dealer's second card is hidden
  • Goal of game is to reach a final hand value higher than the dealer's hand without exceeding 21
  • Hands that exceed 21 will "bust"
  • A hand that busts first loses automatically
  • Player can hit (draw card) or stand (keep hand value) during turn
  • Dealer turn begins after Player turn ends
  • Face card value = 10
  • Ace card value = 1 or 11

House Rules:
  • The dealer must when hand value is under 17
  • Dealer must hit on soft 17 (hand value = 17 and Ace value is 11)
  • Ties will push (no winner)
  • Each round is dealt with a new deck
  • No splits
  • No doubling down
  • No Insurance
